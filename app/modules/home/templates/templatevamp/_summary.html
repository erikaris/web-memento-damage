Techical Notes

Actual Damage
Actual damage is the real damage that happens to your URI. It is computed by list all embedded resources (image, css, javascript, and multimedia), identify which of them are missing, find their importance, and add the importance value together to get the actual damage.
Potential Damage
Potential damage is the damage that could happen if all embedded resources are missing. It is calculated by exactly the same way as actual damage.
Total Damage
Total damage is the ratio between actual damage and potential damage. It ranges from 0 - 1. The value of 1 means your URI is 100% damaged.
Total damage is computed by using this formula:
$Total Damage=\frac{Actual Damage}{Potential Damage}$
What constitute the damage value?

There are 5 components which are considered when computing damage:

  1. Image
  2. Css
  3. Javascript
  4. Multimedia
  5. Text

We added together the important values from the embedded resources to get the value of damaged.
